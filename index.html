Welcome

This site is designed as a compact portfolio for hiring managers, producers, and design leads. The navigation on the left contains a plain resume page followed by project-specific case pages.

My work usually sits between design intent and implementation: defining features, documenting rules and flows, reviewing work in engine, and iterating with art, engineering, production, and QA until a feature is readable and ready to ship.

Please feel free to reach out via direct message on LinkedIn.
