Professional summary

Game designer with shipped and publicly released work across VR, MR, PC, console, multiplayer, simulation, historical strategy, UI, QA, and level design. Primary focus areas are systems design, implementation-facing documentation, and level design. Experienced turning broad concepts into buildable features, clear specifications, reusable briefs, wireframes, tuning frameworks, and practical iteration plans for artists, programmers, producers, and QA.

Areas of focus

  • Systems design: Gameplay loops, progression, economy, rewards, enemy behaviour, player feedback, and balance tuning.
  • Implementation-facing documentation: Feature briefs, wireframes, reusable specs, variable lists, tuning frameworks, and practical handoff material.
  • Level and encounter design: Onboarding, route planning, mission flow, hazard readability, landmarking, pacing, and tactical sightlines.
  • VR / MR gameplay: Comfort-aware interaction, player guidance, tool design, readable hazards, and first-time-user onboarding.
  • Cross-discipline support: Playtesting, QA follow-through, historical reference, implementation review, and production-facing iteration.
