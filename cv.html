Professional summary

I have worked across shipped and public-facing PC and VR projects in historical combat, city-building, creature simulation, cooperative installations, UI-heavy production, and community multiplayer spaces. My work regularly covers feature design, level flow, implementation-ready documentation, historical and visual research, implementation review, playtesting, tuning, and QA follow-through. I also work closely with production, art, engineering, stakeholders, and IP holders to keep design work clear, feasible, and aligned with product goals.

Areas of focus

  • Systems design: Core loops, progression, upgrades, scoring, economy, ecology, onboarding, enemy behavior, and player feedback.
  • Level design: Multiplayer flow, encounter readability, route planning, landmarking, traversal, terrain workflow, and tactical sightlines.
  • VR gameplay: Comfort-aware interaction, tool design, readable hazards, tactile feedback, and onboarding for first-time players.
  • Documentation and integration: Design briefs, wireframes, reference packets, tuning docs, implementation review, and cross-discipline handoff.
  • Leadership and coordination: Delegation, feedback, production planning, milestone support, and requirement management.
