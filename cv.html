Professional summary

Game designer with shipped, publicly available work across VR, MR, PC and console platforms. Wide variety of experience in solo, multiplayer, shooter, adventure, simulation, historical, platformer, strategy and other genres. Primary focus areas are systems and level design, implementation-facing documentation and production support. I have experience turning concepts into deliverables through clear specification documents, handoff briefs, wireframes, and good old fashioned practical iteration. I love working with artists, programmers, producers, and QA and am a reliable member of any team - I believe benevolent delegation is the core tenet of team management.

Skills

Hard skills

  • Systems design: Gameplay loops, core mechanics, progression structure, economy frameworks, hooks and rewards, state trees, feedback loops, and in-engine balance tuning.
  • Level and gameplay design: Onboarding and tutorials, route and encounter planning, mission structure, pacing, hazard readability, tactical sightlines, and heatmap composition.
  • Documentation and technical design: Implementation-facing feature briefs, production wireframes, variable definitions, mockups, and practical handoff material prepared for engineering and art.
  • VR / MR gameplay design: Comfort-aware design, affordances, intuitive tactile guidance, tool-based interaction, and readable immersive level design.
  • Production and implementation support: Playtesting and notetaking, QA validation and logging, implementation review and iteration planning.

Soft skills

  • Cross-discipline collaboration: Works effectively with artists, programmers, producers, and QA to define and produce usable features.
  • Communication: Translates concepts into structured, readable, implementation-ready design specifications with clear deliverables.
  • Problem solving: Every project will propose problems - I am prepared to use practical iteration, user testing, and observation to identify and resolve problems with systems, levels, and the user experience.
  • Reliability and ownership: I am always a dependable contributor. I can help the team carry work from concept through to completion.
  • Adaptability: I can and have worked across multiple genres and platforms. VR, MR, PC, and console projects, and solo, multiplayer, shooter, adventure, simulation, historical, platformer, and strategy work have all been my domain.
