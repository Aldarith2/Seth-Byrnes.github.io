Project description and my role: A large-scale historical multiplayer shooter set during the American Civil War. My work focused on historical level design, route readability, landmarking, terrain composition, and the multiplayer clarity needed to make large battle spaces read under pressure.

Things I did on the project

  • Handled historical research and location reference gathering for map work.
  • Worked through paper design, terrain roughout, multiplayer blockout planning, and terrain pass iteration.
  • Used ridgelines, fences, earthworks, structures, and soft cover to shape movement and sightlines.
  • Supported set dressing, playtesting, and multiplayer iteration so the spaces stayed believable and readable.
