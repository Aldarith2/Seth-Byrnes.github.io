Project description and my role: A WWII bomber simulation built around historical research, mission framing, crew-space readability, and primary-source reference. My role sat between design, narrative support, visual guidance, and cross-discipline coordination.

Things I did on the project

  • Researched aircraft, operations, and reference material using museum and primary-source sources.
  • Produced gameplay and level documentation tied to bomber operations and mission flow.
  • Wrote historically grounded narrative support content.
  • Built visual reference and mockup material to align art and environment work.
