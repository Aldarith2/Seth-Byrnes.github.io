Project description and my role: A mixed-reality habitat and dinosaur-management project built around plants, ecology, progression, player tools, and readable feedback. My work focused on connected systems, onboarding flow, milestone planning, and documentation that could align art, engineering, and production.

Things I did on the project

  • Produced documentation for plant systems, growth rules, placement logic, and ecosystem relationships.
  • Designed dinosaur needs, satisfaction states, buddy systems, trust, levelling, and reward structures.
  • Mapped MR tutorial flow, player tools, and feature onboarding so the experience could teach itself cleanly.
  • Supported milestone planning and wireframing, aligning production goals with design implementation.
