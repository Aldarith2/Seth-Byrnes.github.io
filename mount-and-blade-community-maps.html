Mount & Blade Community Maps

Self-Directed · Level Designer / Community Operator · 2015 - 2018

Store page link: Mod / Forum Page

Release Year: 2015 (ongoing releases through 2018)

Project description and my role: A long-running self-directed multiplayer mapping project for Mount & Blade: With Fire & Sword. The work focused on balance, metrics, lane identity, long-term iteration, and building maps that held up under live community play.

Things I did on the project

  • Designed and iterated on 25+ original multiplayer maps.
  • Overhauled 50+ community maps and supported gameplay modifications.
  • Balanced spaces around the game’s metrics, engagement ranges, movement speed, and mode expectations.
  • Used content quality and variety to help grow a multiplayer server community.
