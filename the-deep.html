Position Summary

VR underwater adventure built around exploration, environmental cleanup, discovery, and transformation. I designed the project in pre-production across core loop definition, player tools, onboarding, progression, hazard design, UI concepts, and implementation-ready documentation. Once the project was live, I kept revising the designs on the fly while testing, configuring, and handling level design in parallel.

Selected Responsibilities

  • Defined and iterated the core gameplay loop around exploration, cleaning, challenge unlocking, upgrades, and forward progression.
  • Designed player tools, HUD concepts, onboarding flow, challenge-area structure, traversal rules, scanning behaviour, and environmental cleanup interactions in pre-production.
  • Authored feature documentation for suit systems, Sonic Laser behaviour, shared energy economy, artifact upgrades, and player feedback loops, then revised those systems live as the project evolved.
  • Supported production planning, in-engine tuning, level design, implementation review, and revision based on internal testing and QA.
