Ownership and Outcome

Early studio work split between First Impact: Rise of a Hero and the separate Onslaught wave-shooter release for Samsung VR. My role combined game design support, level design, progression tuning, enemy-wave planning, QA, and minor in-engine implementation work, giving me hands-on exposure to both content creation and day-to-day development support.

Responsibilities

  • Designed four replayable arena levels with increasing difficulty and helped structure progression and enemy-wave escalation for Onslaught.
  • Worked in QA on First Impact, testing levels, superpowers, and gameplay flow while reporting and helping resolve issues.
  • Maintained and updated design documentation in collaboration with senior designers and integrated art and gameplay assets where needed.
  • Made minor in-engine fixes, supported a junior programmer, and helped tune features during development.
