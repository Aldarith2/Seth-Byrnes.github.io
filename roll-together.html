Ownership and Outcome

Student-studio prototype work spanning party-game readability, obstacle timing, cooperative systems, and public showcase support. This body of work covered Roll Together, CritterBall, and CritterBox, with a focus on turning small prototype ideas into experiences that were understandable quickly in playtests and at events.

Responsibilities

  • Designed cooperative and environmental gameplay for CritterBall and CritterBox, including obstacles, hazards, puzzle structure, and level flow.
  • Supported a co-op concept built for up to 100 phone-connected players and helped shape the experience around fast onboarding and immediate readability.
  • Helped keep interdisciplinary work aligned through clear design language, testing, and shared documentation.
  • Tested across multiple devices and supported public showcases at EGLX and LevelUP.
