Roll Together / CritterBall / CritterBox

Mindwax Games · Game Designer · 2017 - 2018

Store page link: YouTube Demonstration

Trailer file: Embedded local trailer

Release Year: 2018 (prototype demo)

Project description and my role: Prototype multiplayer design work focused on party-game readability, obstacle timing, shared movement, and large-group co-op. This work covered Roll Together, CritterBall, and CritterBox inside a student-studio setting and public showcase context.

Things I did on the project

  • Designed obstacles, levels, and environmental gameplay for CritterBall and CritterBox.
  • Supported a co-op experience capable of up to 100 phone-connected players.
  • Helped facilitate team communication through design language and shared documents.
  • Tested compatibility across devices and supported public showcases at EGLX and LevelUP.
