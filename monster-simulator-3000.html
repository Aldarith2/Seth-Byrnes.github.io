Position Summary

First-person VR kaiju destruction game where the design challenge was making large-scale action feel readable, destructive, and replayable from a close player perspective. I worked on encounter structure, objective logic, scoring, throw behaviour, spawn rules, perk tuning, and level requirements, and I owned the enemy design features on the project from design through implementation oversight, testing, and configuration.

Responsibilities

  • Owned enemy design features end to end, including design documentation, implementation oversight, testing, and release configuration.
  • Defined scoring rules, throw behaviour, spawn logic, perk tuning, and action feedback for first-person destruction gameplay.
  • Structured replay value through event logic, randomisation, escalation beats, and boss-style pressure.
  • Reviewed implementation and tuned action readability, target prioritisation, pacing, and reward cadence through testing.
