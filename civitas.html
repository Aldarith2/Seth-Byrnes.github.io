Position Summary

Historical city-builder developed toward Early Access. I worked as the general design lead across systems design, production planning, wireframes, implementation support, UI and audio prototyping, artist reference, and day-to-day design leadership. The work ranged from concept framing through live production support, with an emphasis on keeping the team aligned around usable design documentation and actionable feedback.

Selected Responsibilities

  • Led design work from concept to Early Access across systems, documentation, production materials, wireframes, UI support, and artist reference.
  • Maintained live documentation and implementation-facing direction while coordinating feedback across disciplines during active production.
  • Delegated work, reviewed execution, and provided actionable feedback to keep the project aligned with product goals and player-facing clarity.
  • Converted playtest findings into improvements to pacing, usability, balance, guidance, and overall presentation.
