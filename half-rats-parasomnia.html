Project description and my role: A survival-horror total conversion for GoldSRC where I worked in QA with a strong focus on issue capture, pacing, level readability, and player-experience notes rather than bug logging alone.

Things I did on the project

  • Playtested the full game and recorded chapter playthroughs for issue tracking.
  • Captured screenshots, location data, reproduction steps, and circumstance notes for bugs.
  • Reported balance issues, cosmetic issues, UI mistakes, and encounter-flow problems.
  • Suggested gameplay and level-layout changes to improve clarity, pacing, and overall feel.
