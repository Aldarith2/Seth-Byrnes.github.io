Position Summary

GoldSRC survival-horror total conversion where I worked in QA with a strong focus on issue capture, pacing, interaction clarity, and player-experience feedback rather than bug logging alone. The role required playing the full build critically and communicating problems in a way the team could act on.

Selected Responsibilities

  • Playtested the full game and documented issues with screenshots, location notes, reproduction steps, and context.
  • Reported bugs across balance, cosmetics, UI, encounter flow, progression, and general usability.
  • Flagged gameplay and level-layout problems that affected readability, pacing, tension, and overall flow.
  • Helped the team improve clarity and moment-to-moment player experience through practical QA feedback.
