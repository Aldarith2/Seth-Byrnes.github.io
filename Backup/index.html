WELCOME

I am a US-based Game Designer with shipped work across VR, PC and consoles. I have worked on more than 11 titles spanning multiple genres.

I am authorized to work in both the United States of America and Canada.

My work is usually focused on feature definition and documentation, iteration, and facilitating art, engineering and production teams to push games until they are readable and ready to ship. I love to learn new skills.

My favorite games tend to be simple in concept but difficult to master, moddable and immersive. I believe that the best games offer players a problem to solve and the toolkit to figure it out - sometimes that toolkit is an SDK.

My Skillset

Level Design

The majority of my work has involved level design. I owned lead design for the entirety of the levels and hub worlds for The Deep and have made more than 20+ original levels for other games, including on randomization and worldgen rulesets as well as historical recreations.

Systems Design

My most recent work has focused on on systems design. I designed the ecological system for DinoHab, core systems on The Deep, and randomization for Monster Simulator 3000. I cover interaction rules, narrative and skills progression, creature behaviors.

Documentation

I have worked on documentation for every project I have worked on. Producing briefs, wireframes, implementation notes, and production-facing spec for artists, programmers, and QA is a core skill every designer must be familiar with.

Configuration

I work closely with the whole team to integrate and configure the designs I spec. I review content in engine and build while adjusting setup, values, pacing, until the feature is readable, playable and fun.

Team Coordination

Team coordination is a major secondary aspect of my skillset. For designs to land, all members of a team must understand the goals. I have also worked with IP holders and stakeholders to define requirements to ensure compliance and recognition as well as success.
